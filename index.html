Providing Subsidized Accommodation to Those in Need

About Us

Mariata Homes is a charity organization dedicated to offering subsidized accommodation to the public. Our mission is to provide a temporary roof for individuals, especially those from lower socio-economic backgrounds. Occasionally, we extend our support to unexpected middle-class clients who find themselves in need of urgent assistance. Our clients typically stay for a few weeks, and we aim to make a positive impact on their lives during their stay.
